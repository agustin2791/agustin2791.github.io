About J & Son Marine

J & Son Marine, Inc. was started just two years ago but our knowledge expands for more than 30 years. Javier started working on private yachts in Acapulco bay and began navigating as a crew member giving the boat maintenance all through coast of Mexico, San Diego, and Los Angeles. With the birth of his first born son Jared he decided to settle in San Diego. Since 1989, Javier has worked on all San Diego bay marinas working on a variety of projects giving him many years of experience. After his son Graduated from San Diego State, they decided to start J & Son Marine to provide boat owner, Marine captains, and brokers an easy, reliable and honest solution to all their boat needs!
